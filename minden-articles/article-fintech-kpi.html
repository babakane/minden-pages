📊 The Fintech KPI Framework

Measuring fintech innovation requires tracking three interconnected dimensions:

📈 Adoption Metrics

Active users, transaction volume, wallet penetration rates

💰 Economic Impact

Financial inclusion index, GDP contribution, job creation

🔒 Compliance & Risk

Fraud rates, KYC completion, regulatory adherence scores

Core KPIs Dashboard PREMIUM

The article provides complete implementation for tracking these essential metrics:

Mobile Money Penetration

Active wallet holders / Total adult population × 100

Transaction Velocity

Average transactions per user per month

Agent Network Density

Agents per 10,000 adults in coverage area

Digital Payment Ratio

Digital transactions / Total payment volume
